Golden rules of extracts for Preference Card:
         Consist of a single .txt file.
         Contain only one piece of data per line.
         Include a unique header, known as an identifier, for each specific piece of information.
         Starting off each new preference card with Preference card 0001 (4 digits), sequential number, e.g. 0001, 00002 etc. 

1: Yes
0: No
